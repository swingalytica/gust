# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in Gust a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of age, background, or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Any form of discrimination

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to **<conduct@swingalytica.com>**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments or ban contributors who violate this Code of Conduct.
